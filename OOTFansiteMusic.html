The Music

The music of The Legend of Zelda is not only memorable but incredibly important to the series as a whole. Even in the early NES games, there was clear attention and effort to create music that would be both memorable and moving for the player, but in Ocarina of Time, Majora's Mask, and Wind Waker, this emphasis on effective orchestration got pushed up to 11.

In no particular order, here are my top 5 favourite songs from Ocarina of Time:

Number 1: The Opening Theme

shivers

Number 2: Zoras Domain

It was hard to find a video for this that wasn't at least 30 minutes long.

That's how much people love this song.

Number 3: Hyrule Field

The sound of adventure

Number 4: Potion Shop

One of the most atmospheric songs in a game that's chock full of them

Number 5: Gerudo Valley

Probably the best song in the game, I used to hang out in Gerudo valley just to listen to the music!
